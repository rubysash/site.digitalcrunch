## 1. What Are Cookies?
Cookies are small text files placed on your device to store data that can be recalled by a web server in the domain that placed the cookie. We use cookies and similar technologies for storing and honoring your preferences and settings, analyzing how our website performs, and fulfilling other legitimate purposes.

## 2. How We Use Cookies
Digital Crunch uses cookies for the following purposes:

* **Analytics:** We use Google Analytics to collect data about how you use our site. This helps us understand which AI tools and technical guides are most valuable to our visitors. This data is saved in aggregate form indefinitely.
* **Marketing & CRM:** Because we use GoHighLevel (GHL) for our marketing automation, cookies may be used to track your interaction with our forms and chatbots. This allows us to provide a more personalized experience when you inquire about our AI implementation services.
* **Performance:** We use cookies to ensure the site loads quickly and that our AI bot interfaces function correctly across different sessions.

## 3. Types of Cookies
* **Necessary Cookies:** Required for the operation of the website (e.g., security and load balancing via Netlify).
* **Analytical/Performance Cookies:** Allow us to recognize and count the number of visitors and see how they move around the site.
* **Targeting/Marketing Cookies:** Record your visit to our site, the pages you have visited, and the links you have followed to assist in our marketing efforts through GHL.

## 4. Third-Party Cookies
Please note that third parties (including, for example, providers of external services like Google Analytics) may also use cookies, over which we have limited control. These cookies are likely to be analytical/performance cookies or targeting cookies.

## 5. Controlling Cookies
Most web browsers automatically accept cookies but provide controls that allow you to block or delete them. 

* **Browser Settings:** You can usually find these settings in the "Options" or "Preferences" menu of your browser.
* **Google Analytics Opt-out:** You can prevent your data from being used by Google Analytics by installing the [Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout).

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the technologies we use or in applicable regulations. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
