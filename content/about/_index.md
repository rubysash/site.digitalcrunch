## Security-First AI Implementation
Digital Crunch is led by **James Fraze**, a professional cybersecurity engineer and architect dedicated to bridging the gap between cutting-edge AI automation and enterprise-grade security.

### The Mission
In an era of rapid AI adoption, most businesses sacrifice security for speed. Digital Crunch focuses on "Hardened AI Infrastructure," ensuring that your AI bots, schemas, and automations are not only efficient but resilient against emerging threats.

### Expertise & Credentials
With a deep technical foundation in defensive engineering and risk mitigation, James brings a "Greybeard" perspective to modern AI workflows—focusing on stable, secure, and scalable implementations.

* **Cybersecurity Engineer:** Extensive experience in implementing vulnerability management and risk mitigation technologies for Fortune 100 companies.
* **Certified Authority:** Holder of **31 IT industry certifications**, ensuring a holistic understanding of the full technology stack—from firewalls and application security to user data and cloud architecture.
* **Academic Grounding:** B.S. in Information Security.
* **Real-World Implementation:** Proven track record in building complex automations, custom scripting, secure application deployment, and related cloud-based deployments.

### Professional Accountability
At Digital Crunch, accountability is the core of our consultancy. We prioritize functional, hardened results over creative speculation. Every AI implementation is vetted against industry best practices to protect client data and operational integrity.

To further mitigate risk for our partners, **Digital Crunch is a fully insured professional consultancy.** We carry comprehensive Professional Liability (Errors & Omissions) insurance specifically tailored for cybersecurity and technology services. This ensures that our high standards of accountability are backed by professional-grade protection for the organizations we serve.
